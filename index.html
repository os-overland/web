Open Source Overland

Open Source Overland is a platform where overlanders and DIY enthusiasts can contribute designs, build, and share modular vehicle upgrades. We aim to provide free designs and plans (open-source CAD files and print files) for custom storage, camp kitchens, and other off-road gear—ready for you to print, cut, modify, and assemble. Your Rig, your Rules — start building today! HTML5 UP.
